How it works

An honest pipeline, not a black box.

1

VIN-decoded cohorts

Every listing is decoded to its true year, trim, and powertrain, then grouped with genuine like-for-like comps, so a wrong powertrain never poisons a cohort.

2

Robust fair-value fits

Prices are fit with an outlier-resistant robust regression we call the "Flow Line," falling back to MSRP-retention curves where a cohort is thin, so no single strange listing drags the line.

3

Verified listings

Trim and powertrain are confirmed against the dealer's own listing, and every record carries the provenance of how it was cleared, shown in the open rather than hidden.

4

Continuous price history

We track every listing over time: days on lot, each price cut, and when it finally sells, so aging and negotiability are measured, not guessed.
